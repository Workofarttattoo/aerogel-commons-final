Synthesis Methods & Protocols

🌾

Agricultural Waste Silica

Rice husk ash → sustainable silica

0.08-0.15 Density (g/cm³)
500-800 Surface Area (m²/g)
$2-10 Cost ($/kg)
85% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Controlled burn rice husks at 500°C for 4 hours to obtain silica ash
2. Acid leach with 2M HCl for 30 min to remove impurities
3. Alkali dissolution with 2M NaOH to form sodium metasilicate
4. pH adjustment to 4-5 for silica sol formation
5. Gelation at 40°C for 1-2 hours
6. Solvent exchange (ethanol) and CO₂ supercritical drying

Equipment needed: Furnace, centrifuge, pH meter, supercritical dryer

Time required: 3-4 days

Scale: 10g to 100g batches

🦠

Microbial Alginate

Bacterial synthesis → biocompatible

0.02-0.08 Density (g/cm³)
200-400 Surface Area (m²/g)
$10-50 Cost ($/kg)
75% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Culture engineered Pseudomonas aeruginosa in LB media at 30°C
2. Ferment for 48 hours to produce alginate extracellularly
3. Centrifuge to harvest alginate-rich supernatant
4. Precipitate alginate with ethanol and recover by centrifugation
5. Dissolve in water and cross-link with 0.1M CaCl₂
6. Freeze-dry at -80°C to obtain aerogel

Equipment needed: Biosafety cabinet, incubator, centrifuge, freeze-dryer

Time required: 4-5 days (including fermentation)

Scale: 5g to 50g batches

⚡

Plasma Titania

Atmospheric plasma → photocatalytic

0.3-0.8 Density (g/cm³)
150-300 Surface Area (m²/g)
$15-40 Cost ($/kg)
60% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Set up atmospheric pressure plasma jet (Ar/O₂ gas mixture)
2. Vaporize TiCl₄ at 25-40°C and mix with carrier gas
3. Ignite plasma (10-15 kV, 10-50 mA) for TiO₂ nanoparticle formation
4. Deposit on heated substrate (150-300°C) for in-situ gelation
5. Control deposition time (10-60 min) for desired thickness
6. Optional annealing at 400°C for crystallization

Equipment needed: Plasma reactor, mass flow controllers, high-voltage power supply

Time required: 1-2 hours per batch

Scale: Lab scale to continuous processing

🧪

Ionic Liquid Carbon

Tunable porosity → energy storage

0.2-0.5 Density (g/cm³)
1000-1500 Surface Area (m²/g)
$20-80 Cost ($/kg)
65% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Dissolve resorcinol in ionic liquid ([EMIM][BF₄]) solution
2. Add formaldehyde and Na₂CO₃ catalyst for polymerization
3. Cure at 85°C for 24 hours to form ionic liquid-templated gel
4. Solvent exchange with acetone to remove water
5. Supercritical CO₂ drying to preserve porous structure
6. Carbonize at 800°C under N₂ for 2 hours

Equipment needed: Magnetic stirrer, oven, supercritical dryer, tube furnace

Time required: 4-5 days

Scale: 5g to 50g batches

🔥

Polymer SiC

High-temperature ceramics

0.3-0.6 Density (g/cm³)
100-300 Surface Area (m²/g)
$50-200 Cost ($/kg)
70% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Dissolve polycarbosilane in toluene with dicumyl peroxide
2. Cast into molds and cross-link at 120°C for 2 hours
3. Post-cure at 200°C for thermal treatment
4. Solvent exchange with acetone and supercritical drying
5. Pyrolysis at 1000°C under argon atmosphere
6. Optional oxidation for SiC-SiO₂ composite formation

Equipment needed: Tube furnace, supercritical dryer, inert gas system

Time required: 5-7 days

Scale: 2g to 20g batches

🔬

Nanoparticle Metal Oxide

Bottom-up multifunctional assembly

0.1-0.3 Density (g/cm³)
200-500 Surface Area (m²/g)
$30-100 Cost ($/kg)
80% Yield (%)

Synthesis Steps:

1. Functionalize ZnO and Al₂O₃ nanoparticles with APTES
2. Disperse in ethanol using ultrasonic homogenization
3. Mix nanoparticle dispersions in controlled ratios
4. Adjust pH to 4-5 for electrostatic assembly
5. Gelation at room temperature with gentle stirring
6. Supercritical drying to preserve nanoparticle network

Equipment needed: Ultrasonic homogenizer, pH meter, supercritical dryer

Time required: 3-4 days

Scale: 5g to 100g batches
