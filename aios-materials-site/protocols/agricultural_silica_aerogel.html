🌾 Agricultural Silica Aerogel Synthesis Protocol

Transform rice husk ash into high-performance silica aerogels using sustainable waste valorization techniques.

Overview

This protocol describes the synthesis of silica aerogels from rice husk ash, providing a sustainable and cost-effective alternative to traditional TEOS-based methods.

Materials Required

Precursors

  • Rice husk ash (RHA) - 10g per batch
  • 2M Hydrochloric acid (HCl) - 100mL
  • 2M Sodium hydroxide (NaOH) - 50mL
  • Distilled water - 500mL

Equipment

  • Magnetic stirrer hotplate
  • Centrifuge (4000 rpm)
  • Vacuum filtration setup
  • pH meter
  • Supercritical CO₂ dryer
  • Muffle furnace (500°C)

Step-by-Step Procedure

1. Rice Husk Ash Preparation (2 hours): Collect rice husks, wash thoroughly, dry at 80°C, controlled burn at 500°C for 4 hours, grind to fine powder.
2. Acid Leaching (1 hour): Add 10g ash to 100mL 2M HCl, stir at 80°C for 30 min, centrifuge, wash with water, dry at 80°C.
3. Alkaline Dissolution (2 hours): Add 50mL 2M NaOH to leached ash, stir at 90°C for 1 hour, filter, adjust pH to 7.0.
4. Silica Sol Formation (30 minutes): Acidify solution to pH 2-3, stir vigorously for 15 min, age at room temperature.
5. Gelation (2 hours): Add organic acid catalyst, pour into molds, cure at 40°C for 1-2 hours.
6. Solvent Exchange (24 hours): Replace water with ethanol (3 exchanges), final exchange with CO₂.
7. Drying (4-8 hours): Supercritical CO₂ drying at 40°C, 1200 psi, slow depressurization.

Characterization

  • Density: 0.08-0.15 g/cm³
  • Surface Area: 500-800 m²/g
  • Porosity: 90-95%
  • Thermal Conductivity: 0.015-0.025 W/m·K

Expected Results

  • Yield: 60-80% based on silica content
  • Appearance: White, translucent monolith
  • Cost: $2-10 per kg
  • Processing Time: 3-4 days

Safety Considerations

  • Handle acids/bases with proper PPE
  • Work in fume hood for acid treatments
  • Silica dust precautions
  • High pressure supercritical drying requires training

Applications

  • Thermal insulation
  • Catalysis support
  • Environmental cleanup
  • Acoustic insulation
