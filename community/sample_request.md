# Aerogel Commons Sample Distribution Program

## Overview
Free aerogel samples available to qualified researchers to accelerate materials science research and development.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Academic Researchers
- Faculty, post-doctoral researchers, or graduate students
- Active research program in materials science, chemistry, physics, or related fields
- Publication record or research proposal demonstrating need

### Industry Researchers
- R&D scientists at established companies
- Startup founders with technical background
- Clear application for aerogel materials in products/services

### Government Researchers
- Scientists at national laboratories or research institutions
- Research focused on energy, environmental, or defense applications

## Application Process

### Step 1: Submit Application
Fill out the online application form at [aerogelcommons.org/samples](https://aerogelcommons.org/samples) including:

- **Personal Information**: Name, affiliation, contact details
- **Research Background**: CV or resume, publication list
- **Research Proposal**: 1-2 page description of how samples will be used
- **Ethical Statement**: Commitment to acknowledge Aerogel Commons in publications

### Step 2: Review Process
- Applications reviewed within 2 weeks
- Technical merit and research impact evaluation
- Priority given to innovative applications and underrepresented researchers

### Step 3: Sample Shipment
- Approved applicants receive samples via express shipping
- Typical quantities: 1-5 grams per material
- Materials include: silica, carbon, alginate, and specialty aerogels

## Available Materials

### Standard Aerogels
- **Agricultural Silica Aerogel**: Rice husk-derived, thermal insulation
- **Microbial Alginate Aerogel**: Biocompatible, biomedical applications
- **Ionic Liquid Carbon Aerogel**: High surface area, energy storage
- **Plasma Titania Aerogel**: Photocatalytic, environmental cleanup

### Specialty Formulations
- **Composite Aerogels**: Multi-material combinations
- **Functionalized Aerogels**: Surface-modified for specific applications
- **High-Performance Variants**: Enhanced mechanical/thermal properties

## Usage Guidelines

### Research Use Only
- Samples provided for research purposes only
- Commercial use requires separate licensing agreement
- Redistribution of samples not permitted

### Reporting Requirements
- Share characterization data with community
- Publish results acknowledging Aerogel Commons
- Contribute improvements back to open-source protocols

### Safety & Handling
- Follow provided safety data sheets
- Handle nanomaterials with appropriate precautions
- Dispose of waste according to local regulations
